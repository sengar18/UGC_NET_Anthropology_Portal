# Research Methodology Cheat Sheet

## Qualitative Methods
- **Ethnography**: In-depth study of cultures. Key method: Participant observation.
- **Interviews**: Structured, semi-structured, and unstructured.
- **Focus Groups**: Group discussions led by a moderator.

## Quantitative Methods
- **Surveys & Questionnaires**: Collecting standardized data from large populations.
- **Statistical Analysis**: Descriptive (mean, median, mode) vs Inferential (t-tests, ANOVA).
- **Sampling**: Random, stratified, purposive, snowball.

## Key Concepts
- **Validity**: Does the tool measure what it claims to measure?
- **Reliability**: Consistency of the measurement.
- **Ethics**: Informed consent, anonymity, do no harm.
